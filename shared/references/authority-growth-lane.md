# Authority And Growth Lane

Use this lane for website growth, authority building, SEO, AEO, GEO, AI answer visibility, content strategy, and conversion work tied to a WordPress product or site.

## Scope

This is not generic blog writing. Use it for evidence-backed growth work:

- topic, entity, and authority strategy,
- citation-worthiness and AI answer visibility,
- internal-linking and content moat planning,
- conversion path and CTA improvements,
- analytics, Search Console, and search-intent feedback loops,
- public-safe competitor or ecosystem research,
- issue-ready website, docs, and content tasks.

## Outputs

Produce one of these:

- growth brief with evidence and expected impact,
- focused GitHub issue(s) for content/site/product changes,
- conversion experiment with measurement plan,
- docs or information-architecture recommendations,
- content execution routed to `content-writer`,
- website implementation routed to `wp-site-expert`,
- search-positioning analysis routed to `seo-positioning-optimizer`.

## Guardrails

- Keep claims evidence-backed; do not promise traffic, rankings, or citations.
- Keep public GitHub wording competitor-neutral unless the owner explicitly approves names.
- If the task changes visible design, include before screenshots in issues and after screenshots or proof gaps in PRs.
- Route product-specific prioritization through the PO; route cross-product prioritization through CTO.
